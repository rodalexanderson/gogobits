Dubbing and Voice-Over

Voice-over is changing the voice to another language (sometimes it could be the same language) in a video where a person is not visible. Corporate videos, in-house training, and descriptive videos are examples of voice-overs.

Dubbing is changing the voice to another language but matching the person speaking in the video. Voice tone, timing, and emotions are essential for quality.
